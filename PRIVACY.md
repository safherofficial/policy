# Safe(H)er Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

Safe(H)er is designed to protect victims of violence, stalking, and emergencies. Your privacy and safety are our top priority. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Data We Collect

* **Account:** email, name, phone (optional), password hash (bcrypt).
* **Location:** latitude, longitude, reverse-geocoded address — only when you press SOS, share via Circle, or enable Background Mode.
* **Contacts:** names, phone numbers, and email addresses that you explicitly save or import from your device.
* **Evidence:** audio recordings you create in the Vault, stored as Base64-encoded data.
* **Device Signals:** battery level and connectivity status at the time an SOS is triggered.

## What We Do NOT Collect

* No background location without your explicit consent.
* No advertising identifiers or third-party trackers.
* No microphone recording outside the SOS flow that you initiate.
* No access to your device contacts unless you choose **"Import from Phone"**.

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used **exclusively** to provide Safe(H)er's safety features, including sending SOS alerts, sharing your location with your trusted Circle, and securely storing the evidence you choose to record.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

## Data Sharing

* Your trusted contacts receive your SOS message (via SMS or WhatsApp, depending on your preference).
* Members of your Circle can view your live location only while location sharing is active.
* We **never** sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

## Security

* Passwords are hashed using **bcrypt**.
* Your PIN is stored **locally** using your device's secure storage (Secure Enclave / SecureStore).
* All communication between the app and our servers is protected with **HTTPS/TLS encryption**.
* Biometric authentication is managed entirely by your operating system. Safe(H)er never accesses or stores your fingerprint or facial recognition data.

## Data Retention

You can delete your emergency events, stored evidence, and trusted contacts at any time.

If you delete your account, all associated personal data will be permanently removed within **30 days**, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, export, and delete your personal data.

For privacy-related requests, contact us at **[safherofficial@gmail.com](mailto:safherofficial@gmail.com)**.

## Children

Safe(H)er is intended for users aged **16 and older** (18+ in jurisdictions where required by law).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any material changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated through the application.
